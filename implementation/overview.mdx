Superatom deploys within your infrastructure as a set of Docker containers. There is no cloud instance, no data migration, and no third-party environment. The platform connects to your existing databases and serves users from within your network.

---

## Approach

Superatom's **automated semantic modeling** handles what traditional BI tools require consultants to do manually. When a data source is connected, the system:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Schema Analysis">
    Analyzes the database schema and profiles the data automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Business-Term Mapping">
    Proposes mappings between database columns and business terms your team actually uses.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Queryable Model">
    Builds a queryable semantic model so teams can start asking questions immediately.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Continuous Refinement">
    Improves through usage and structured feedback as domain experts capture tribal knowledge.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Timeline at a Glance

Core implementation takes **6-8 weeks**, with an additional 4-week optimization phase.

| Week | Phase | What Happens |
|------|-------|--------------|
| 1-2 | Deploy and Connect | Platform deployed. Chat Agent live. Priority questions validated. |
| 2-4 | Build Knowledge and Refine | Semantic model enriched. Tribal knowledge captured. Permissions configured. |
| 4-6 | Dashboards, Reports, and Workflows | Dashboards shared. Reports scheduled. Workflows configured. Mobile enabled. |
| 6-8 | Go-Live and Activation | Full production. Autonomous monitoring live. All teams onboarded. |
| 8-12 | Optimization and Expansion | Performance tuned. Additional sources connected. Admin trained. |

## Phase Overview

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Phase 1: Deploy and Connect" icon="plug">
    Platform running on your infrastructure with initial data sources connected and the Chat Agent answering questions. Power users validate answers against live data.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Phase 2: Build Knowledge and Refine" icon="brain">
    The semantic model is enriched with organizational context through structured feedback and tribal knowledge capture. Common questions reach high accuracy.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Phase 3: Dashboards, Reports, and Workflows" icon="chart-mixed">
    Operational dashboards, scheduled reports, automated workflows, and mobile access are configured and deployed.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Phase 4: Go-Live and Activation" icon="circle-check">
    Full production deployment. Autonomous monitoring is activated. All user groups are onboarded across the organization.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Phase 5: Optimization and Expansion" icon="chart-line-up">
    Performance optimization, expanded use cases, stress testing, and knowledge transfer to your admin team.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Note>
  Superatom deploys entirely within your infrastructure. No customer data leaves your network. The only outbound connection is HTTPS to the LLM provider, which can be proxied or replaced with a self-hosted model for air-gapped environments.
</Note>
