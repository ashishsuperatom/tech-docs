Get Superatom connected to your data and running your first analysis.

## Prerequisites

- Admin access to your Superatom workspace
- Database connection details (host, port, credentials)
- About 15 minutes

## Setup Steps

<Steps>

1. **Access Your Workspace**

   Log in at `your-org.superatom.ai` with your credentials.

2. **Connect a Data Source**

   Navigate to **Knowledge Base → Data Sources** and click **Add Source**.

   Enter your connection details:
   - Host and port
   - Database name
   - Username and password
   - SSL settings

3. **Wait for Analysis**

   Superatom will analyze your schema automatically. This typically takes 2-5 minutes for most databases.

4. **Ask Your First Question**

   Go to the **Chat Agent** and type a question in plain English:

   > "What were our total sales last month?"

5. **Explore the Results**

   The AI will:
   - Generate the appropriate query
   - Execute it against your data
   - Display results with the optimal visualization

</Steps>

<Aside>
You can bookmark useful queries and add them to dashboards for quick access later.
</Aside>
