## Welcome to Superatom

This guide walks you through setting up Superatom for your organization.

## Prerequisites

- Admin access to your Superatom workspace
- Database connection details
- 30 minutes of setup time

## Step 1: Initial Setup

1. **Access Your Workspace**

   Log in at your-org.superatom.ai

  1. **Complete Onboarding**

   Follow the guided setup wizard

  1. **Invite Team Members**

   Add colleagues who will use the platform

## Step 2: Connect Data

See [Connecting Data](/guides/connecting-data) for detailed instructions.

## Step 3: Configure Knowledge

Add organization-specific knowledge:

1. Business term definitions
2. Calculation rules
3. Access policies

## Step 4: Start Analyzing

Begin asking questions in the Chat Agent.
