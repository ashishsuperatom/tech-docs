## Enterprise Intelligence Across Your Organization

Superatom applies across industries, organizational roles, and common enterprise scenarios. Whether you are optimizing a supply chain, managing financial risk, or preparing for a board meeting, Superatom connects to your existing data sources, understands your organizational context, and delivers actionable insights in seconds.

Browse by industry to see how Superatom maps to your domain, or explore by role to understand how different teams interact with the platform day-to-day.
