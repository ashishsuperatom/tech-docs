## Report Types

| Type | Description |
|------|-------------|
| Scheduled | Run on a recurring basis |
| Triggered | Run when conditions are met |
| Ad-hoc | Generated on demand |

## Creating a Scheduled Report

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Reports">
    Navigate to Reports / AI Analyst
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Report">
    Describe what you want to analyze
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Schedule">
    Set frequency and timing
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Recipients">
    Choose who receives the report
  </Step>
  <Step title="Activate">
    Turn on the schedule
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Schedule Options

| Frequency | Examples |
|-----------|----------|
| Daily | Every day at 7 AM |
| Weekly | Every Monday morning |
| Monthly | First of the month |
| Quarterly | Start of each quarter |

## Delivery Methods

- Email with PDF attachment
- Email with link
- Slack channel
- Microsoft Teams
- Webhook

## Best Practices

- Include executive summary
- Keep focused on key metrics
- Test with small group first
- Review and iterate
